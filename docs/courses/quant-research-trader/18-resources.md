???+ "Mathematics & Statistics"
    1. [Options, Futures, and Other Derivatives by John Hull](https://www.amazon.com/Options-Futures-Other-Derivatives-10th/dp/013447208X) - Derivatives bible
    2. [Quantitative Finance by Paul Wilmott](https://www.amazon.com/Paul-Wilmott-Introduces-Quantitative-Finance/dp/0470319585) - Comprehensive quant finance

???+ "Trading & Strategies"

    2. [Quantitative Value by Wesley Gray](https://www.amazon.com/Quantitative-Value-Practitioners-Intelligent-Investing/dp/1118328077) - Value investing quant approach
    3. [Pairs Trading by Ganapathy Vidyamurthy](https://www.amazon.com/Pairs-Trading-Quantitative-Approaches-Strategies/dp/0471460672) - Pairs trading strategies
    4. [Market Microstructure Theory by Maureen O'Hara](https://www.amazon.com/Market-Microstructure-Theory-Maureen-Hara/dp/0631207610) - Market structure

## üí° Key Takeaways

- **Papers**: Read foundational papers to understand theory
- **Tools**: Practice with free tools before investing in paid platforms

- **Practice**: Build projects and paper trade to gain experience
- **Continuous Learning**: Quant finance evolves rapidly - stay updated
